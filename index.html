FAQ

Questions, answered

Is it free to apply?

Yes — 100% free. If anyone asks you to pay to apply or perform, that's not us.

Do I need prior experience?

No. We care about the act, not the résumé. First-timers with something real to bring are very welcome.

What if I'm not selected?

There's always a next edition. Lineup decisions come down to fit and timing for that specific show, not talent alone — apply again.

When will I hear back?

Shortlisted applicants are contacted directly ahead of the event date. If you don't hear from us, assume next time.

Do I need a professional video?

Not at all. A clear phone recording that shows your actual performance is more useful to us than a polished edit that hides it.
